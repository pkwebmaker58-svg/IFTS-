Frequently Asked Questions
1 What is Filer Registration in Pakistan?
Filer Registration is the process of becoming an Active Taxpayer in Pakistan by obtaining an NTN, filing an Income Tax Return with FBR, and becoming eligible for inclusion in the Active Taxpayer List (ATL).
2What is the Active Taxpayer List (ATL)?
The ATL is an official list maintained by FBR that contains the names of taxpayers who have filed their income tax returns and qualify for reduced withholding tax rates in Pakistan.
3Is NTN Registration included in this service?
Yes. NTN Registration is included where required. If you do not already have an NTN, IFTS will assist you in obtaining one before proceeding with filer registration.
4Is ATL Activation included in this service?
Yes. ATL Activation assistance is included in all packages. IFTS guides clients through the process required to become an active filer with FBR.
5Can freelancers register as filers in Pakistan?
Yes. Freelancers, online service providers, remote workers, and digital professionals can apply for Filer Registration and become compliant taxpayers in Pakistan.
6Is MFA linking included?
Yes. MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) linking support is included to help clients maintain secure access to their FBR IRIS account and future tax compliance.
7What is the difference between Basic and Standard packages?
Basic is designed for individuals with simple tax profiles, while Standard includes wealth statement preparation, asset review, vehicle declaration support, property guidance, and foreign remittance review.
8Can I declare property in the Basic package?
No. Property declaration and related wealth statement matters are handled under the Standard or Premium Package depending on complexity.
9Are government penalties included?
No. Government taxes, FBR penalties, ATL surcharges, and any official charges are separate unless specifically mentioned in writing.
10Will I receive the Taxpayer Registration Certificate?
Yes. Clients receive the official FBR Taxpayer Registration Certificate (TRC) along with related registration and filing documents.
11How long does filer registration take?
Normally, Filer Registration takes between 2 to 6 working days depending on the selected package, document completion, and complexity of the case.
12Is hard copy delivery available?
Yes. Digital documents are delivered via WhatsApp and email, while hard copy delivery may also be available where applicable.
13Will my information remain confidential?
Yes. All client information, tax records, financial details, and supporting documents are handled through a secure and confidential workflow.
14Can overseas remittances be handled?
Yes. Overseas remittances can be reviewed and properly reported under the Standard and Premium packages.
15Is business registration included?
No. Business NTN Registration is a separate service. This service is designed specifically for Individual, Salaried, Pensioner, and Freelancer Filer Registration.
16What happens if my case becomes complex?
If your case involves multiple properties, foreign assets, extensive banking transactions, or advanced tax matters, IFTS may recommend the Premium Package for detailed review and compliance support.
17Why is the Premium package priced at Rs. 15,000?
The Rs. 15,000 amount is an Initial Expert Review Fee for complex tax matters. It covers professional assessment, compliance review, and case evaluation before a final quotation is issued.
18Will the Rs. 15,000 be adjusted later?
Yes. If you proceed with IFTS after receiving the final quotation, the Rs. 15,000 Initial Review Fee is adjusted against your final service invoice.
19Can I submit FBR notices under Premium?
Yes. The Premium Package includes professional review of FBR notices, compliance notices, and other tax-related correspondence where applicable.
20Can foreign assets and income be reviewed?
Yes. Foreign assets, foreign income, overseas investments, and international financial matters can be reviewed under the Premium Package.
21Is there a fixed final price in Premium?
No. The final service fee depends on case complexity, number of assets, compliance requirements, and the level of professional work required after review.
22What if I do not proceed after Premium review?
The Initial Expert Review Fee is non-refundable because professional review, assessment, and recommendations have already been provided by IFTS experts.
23Will I receive a quotation before filing?
Yes. A detailed quotation is issued after the expert review process. No filing or compliance work begins until the quotation is approved by the client.
24Is Priority Processing included in Premium?
Yes. After the expert review is completed and the final quotation is accepted, the case is processed on priority and typically completed within 1–2 working days.
25Who should choose Premium?
The Premium Package is ideal for taxpayers with multiple properties, foreign assets, foreign income, high-value transactions, FBR notices, complex wealth statements, compliance risks, or advanced tax advisory requirements.
