Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is a Single Member Company (SMC)?
An SMC is a private company owned by a single shareholder with limited liability protection and separate legal identity.
2. What is included in your SMC registration service?
Our service includes:
• SECP company incorporation
• Name reservation
• MOA/AOA drafting
• NTN registration
• Branding setup
• Legal documentation
• Digital business setup
(depending on package selection)
3. Is SECP official fee included in the package?
No. SECP official fee is charged separately and depends on the authorized capital of the company.
4. How many directors are required in an SMC?
Only one owner/shareholder is required. However, one nominee director is also appointed as per SECP requirements.
5. Can I convert my SMC into a Private Limited Company later?
Yes. An SMC can be converted into a Private Limited Company in the future if additional shareholders/directors are added.
6. What documents are required for SMC registration?
You need:
• CNIC copy
• Proposed company names
• Office address
• Business activity details
• Contact information
• Share capital information
7. How long does SMC registration take?
Normally:
• Basic: 5–8 Working Days
• Standard: 7–10 Working Days
• Premium: 10–15 Working Days
8. Will I receive NTN after registration?
Yes. FBR NTN registration is included in all packages.
9. Is trademark registration included in the package?
Trademark screening guidance is included, but official IPO trademark filing charges are separate.
